FAQ

Questions, answered

Where can I import recipes from?
Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, Facebook, Reddit and just about any recipe website. You can also scan a cookbook page or photo, paste a link, or generate a recipe with AI.
How does it pull recipes from videos?
Ingredio reads the actual video — not just the caption — to extract the real ingredients and steps, then formats them into a clean, structured recipe in seconds.
Does it build a grocery list automatically?
Absolutely. Add recipes to your list and Ingredio combines the ingredients and sorts them by category, so shopping is quick and nothing gets missed.
What devices does it work on?
Ingredio is built for iPhone. Your recipes sync across your devices when you're signed in.
